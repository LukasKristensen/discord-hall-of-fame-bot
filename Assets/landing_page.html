Turn your server's best reactions into legends. Automatically save and showcase your community's top moments with full media support and conversation context.

🌟 Features that Shine

🔹 Multi-Emoji Magic: Any emoji can trigger immortality.

🔹 Media Support: Images and videos travel with the post.

🔹 Context Preservation: Keeps replies visible for the full story.

🔹 Smart Scaling: Thresholds adjust to your server's size automatically.

🚀 Quick Start Guide

1. Invite: Add the bot to your server.

2. Permissions: Ensure it can see your target Hall of Fame channel.

3. Relax: The bot handles the rest! It sets a threshold based on your member count automatically.

⚙️ Advanced Configuration

🔸 /set_reaction_threshold — Override the auto-scaling goal. 🔸 /calculation_method — Switch between unique users or total reactions. 🔸 /allow_messages_in_hof_channel — Toggle channel posting. 🔸 /get_server_config — See everything at a glance.

🔒 Emoji Whitelisting

Control exactly which reactions count toward the Hall of Fame:

🔸 /custom_emoji_check_logic — Toggle whitelist mode. 🔸 /whitelist_emoji — Add approved reactions. 🔸 /unwhitelist_emoji — Remove reactions.
